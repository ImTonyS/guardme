What & Who is GuardMe?

GuardMe, a visionary project with a mission to aid others, was created with a specific purpose in mind. Alzheimer's, a widely recognized disease, relentlessly impacts lives, targeting the very essence of individuals and their cognitive well-being. The project envisions a future where innovative solutions and support systems safeguard against the challenges posed by Alzheimer's, reflecting a commitment to enhancing the quality of life for those affected by this pervasive neurological condition.
